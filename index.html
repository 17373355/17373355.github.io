About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the State Key Lab of CAD & CG, Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Xiaowei Zhou, Sida Peng, and Hujun Bao. My research lies at the intersection of Computer Vision and Graphics, with a primary focus on generative human motion synthesis and physics-plausible behavior modeling.

Prior to ZJU, I earned my M.Sc. with Distinction in Computer Graphics from University College London (UCL) and a B.S. in Computer Science from Beihang University. Currently, I am dedicated to bridging the gap between raw human motion data and expressive, high-fidelity digital character animation.
